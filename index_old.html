About Me

Hello! My name is Abdoulfatah Abdillahi, a Data Science and AI master's student at San Francisco State University.

I'm a researcher passionate about bridging biology and computer science.

My work focuses on predicting antibiotic resistance in E. coli using machine learning. Over the past two years at CodeLab, I've helped build a large-scale pangenome dataset and now develop and compare models to better understand how genetic variation shapes resistance patterns.

Driven by curiosity and a commitment to impact, I aim to make biological insights more accessible and actionable ‚Äî transforming complex genomic data into discoveries that matter.
